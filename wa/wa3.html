Overviews

Soccer is the most popular sport in the world. Called "football" in other parts of the world, it is played in every country. Involving 2 goals and a ball used at the feet, players are not to use their hands in the field of play unless they are a goalkeeper. Soccer is played from ages 4+, while being a large part of culture in cities and countries.
