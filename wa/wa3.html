Overviews

Soccer is the most popular sport in the world. Called "football" in other parts of the world, it is played in every country. Involving 2 goals and a ball used at the feet, players are not to use their hands in the field of play unless they are a goalkeeper.

Game Format

U9-U10: 7 versus 7 with 25 minute halves

U11-U12: 9 versus 9 with 30 minute halves

U13-U14: 11 versus 11 with 35 minute halves

U15-U16: 11 versus 11 with 40 minute halves

U17+: 11 versus 11 with 45 minute halves
